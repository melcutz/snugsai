Frequently asked questions

Is Snugs medical advice or for emergencies? ▾
Snugs.ai provides informational guidance only and is not a substitute for professional medical, psychological, or safety advice. If you believe your child or anyone is in danger, call your local emergency number immediately.
How do you use my data? ▾
Today, we collect limited information (like your email) to manage the waitlist and respond to inquiries. If features process audio/video, we will clearly explain what's used, how it's processed, and provide controls. See our Privacy Policy for details.
Pricing and launch timing ▾
We're preparing early access and will share pricing before launch. Join the waitlist to get priority invites and updates. We'll email details as soon as we open access.
Which devices are supported? ▾
The website supports modern browsers on desktop and mobile. For AI features that use camera/microphone, you'll need a compatible device and to grant permissions. We aim to support recent iOS and Android versions and the latest Chrome, Safari, and Edge.
