Frequently asked questions

Is Snugs safe for my baby? ▾
Absolutely. Snugs provides parenting advice, not medical diagnosis. We cite credible sources and include prominent medical disclaimers. Your data is encrypted and private. For medical emergencies or health concerns, always consult your pediatrician.
Can AI really replace my pediatrician? ▾
No—and we'd never claim it can. Snugs is designed to complement your pediatrician, not replace them. Think of Snugs as your 24/7 parenting advisor for everyday questions ("Why won't my baby nap?") that don't require medical appointments. For health concerns, diagnosis, or emergencies, always see a real doctor.
Will Snugs work for MY baby? ▾
Yes! Snugs is trained on child development from newborn to 15 years. Whether your baby is 3 days old or 3 years old, Snugs tailors advice to their exact age, temperament, feeding style, and developmental stage. Try the 14-day free trial to see for yourself—no credit card required.
What if I'm not tech-savvy? ▾
Snugs is designed to be as simple as texting a friend. Just type (or speak) your question and get an answer. No complicated menus, no confusing settings. Our onboarding takes 2 minutes and walks you through everything. If you can send a text message, you can use Snugs.
How accurate are Snugs' answers? ▾
Snugs uses Google's Gemini AI with "grounding technology"—which means answers are based on credible, real-world parenting sources (pediatric guidelines, child development research, trusted parenting experts). Snugs cites sources so you can verify. That said, AI can make mistakes, which is why we emphasize: use Snugs as a helpful guide, not a substitute for professional medical advice.
What happens to my data? ▾
Your data is encrypted, stored securely on Cloudflare's infrastructure, and never sold or shared with third parties. You own your data—you can export it or delete your account anytime. We're GDPR-compliant and take privacy seriously. See our Privacy Policy for details.
Can I cancel anytime? ▾
Yes! Cancel anytime from your account settings. If you cancel during your trial, you won't be charged. If you cancel after subscribing, you'll keep access until the end of your billing period. No cancellation fees, no hassle.
Do you offer refunds? ▾
We offer a 14-day free trial so you can try Snugs risk-free before paying. If you're not satisfied after subscribing, contact us at support@snugs.ai within 30 days for a full refund.
Can I use Snugs for multiple children? ▾
Yes! Create separate profiles for each child. Snugs will tailor advice to each baby's age and needs. Perfect for families with multiple kids or caregivers managing twins.
