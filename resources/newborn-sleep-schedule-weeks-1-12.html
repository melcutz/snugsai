Newborn Sleep Schedule: Weeks 1–12

Not medical advice. Every baby is unique—use as a gentle guide.

Use these typical ranges to set realistic expectations. Follow your baby's cues over the clock.

Weeks 1–2

  • • Wake window: 35–50 min
  • • Naps/day: 6–8+
  • • Night feeds: on demand

Weeks 3–4

  • • Wake window: 45–60 min
  • • Naps/day: 6–7
  • • Night feeds: on demand

Weeks 5–8

  • • Wake window: 60–75 min
  • • Naps/day: 5–6
  • • Longer stretch may emerge

Weeks 9–12

  • • Wake window: 75–90 min
  • • Naps/day: 4–5
  • • Bedtime routine: 10–20 min

Gentle Routine Template

Feed → Burp → Awake time (low stimulation) → Wind‑down → Sleep. Keep lights low after sunset, and use consistent cues.
